Hi, I’m Tim Lockridge

I am Associate Professor and Director of Graduate Studies in the Department of English at Miami University. I am also an affiliate faculty member in the Department of Emerging Technology in Business and Design.

My book, Writing Workflows: Beyond Word Processing (written with Derek Van Ittersum), was published by the University of Michigan Press in 2020 and is available in two open access formats: epub and web-based. Derek and I also published a textbook chapter based on our research, which is available in the Writing Spaces: Volume 4 open access textbook.

I am also

  • Senior Editor of Computers and Composition Digital Press;
  • developer of Rhetorlist;
  • building a digital archive of Computers and Writing conference programs; and
  • working on a new book project.

I also write fiction and poetry. A few of my published poems include:

  • Survival Tips for a Parallel Universe — a featured author chapbook published by the Mid-American Review.
  • “New Ruin, Old World” at DIAGRAM
  • “Governing Bodies” at Verse Daily
  • “On Realizing I Tend To End With Nature Imagery” at Verse Daily
  • “A Brief Meditation on Movement” at qarrtsiluni

I don't post much on social media, but I do occasionally write on my personal micro.blog—mostly about music and running.
